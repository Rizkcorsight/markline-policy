Markline — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Markline collects nothing. It has no account, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. There is no backend server; the bundled survey-mark database and your recovery logs stay on your device.

Data stored on your device

The recoveries you log (found / not-found / destroyed, plus any condition, note, or photo you add) are stored only on your device. They are never transmitted to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes them.

Location

If you allow it, Markline uses your location to show the survey marks near you — to center the map and to sort the Nearby list by distance. Your location is used only on your device for this; it is never stored or uploaded.

Maps & internet

The app and its survey-mark database work fully offline. When you are online, map tiles are provided by Apple Maps (on iPhone, iPad, and Mac) or Google Maps (on Android) and are subject to those providers' terms. You may also optionally download an individual mark's full datasheet from the public NOAA NGS website; it is then cached on your device for offline reuse.

Survey-mark data

The bundled survey-mark database is from the NOAA National Geodetic Survey and is in the public domain. It ships inside the app for offline use. Markline is not affiliated with or endorsed by NOAA or the National Geodetic Survey.

Children

Markline does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.
