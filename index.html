Markline

Every U.S. survey mark in your pocket — bundled for offline field reference.

Markline is a field companion for benchmark hunting — finding and logging the brass disks and survey marks placed across the country by the National Geodetic Survey.

  • 795,000+ NGS marks, bundled for offline field reference
  • A map of marks near you, color-coded by last reported condition
  • A distance-sorted “Nearby” list — the closest marks to where you are
  • The complete datasheet for every mark — type, coordinates, elevation, recovery history
  • Log your recoveries (found / not-found / destroyed) with photos and notes
  • Browse by state and county, or search by PID or designation

Start with a 3-day full-access free trial; after the trial, a one-time purchase unlocks the app permanently. No account. No subscription. No ads. Nothing is collected — your finds stay on your device. Survey-mark data is from the NOAA National Geodetic Survey (public domain). Markline is not affiliated with or endorsed by NOAA or the National Geodetic Survey.
